# StoneVision AI — Business Registration Guide
## How to Legally Protect Your Business in India

---

## HONEST ANSWER FIRST

You have **three things to protect**:

1. **Your personal assets** — if a customer sues you, can they take your house?
2. **Your brand name "StoneVision AI"** — can a competitor open "StoneVision AI Madurai"?
3. **Your software and code** — can someone copy your app and resell it?

Each needs a different registration. Here's exactly what to do.

---

## PART 1: BUSINESS STRUCTURE
### Which one should YOU register?

---

### OPTION A — Sole Proprietorship (Do NOT use this for StoneVision)

**What it is:** You trade in your own name. No separate company exists.

**Cost to register:** ₹0 — just your GST registration (₹0) and a current account.

**The problem for StoneVision:**
- **Unlimited personal liability.** If a customer claims your AI caused them a ₹20 lakh loss and sues you — they can attach your personal bank account, your property, your savings. There is NO legal wall between you and the business.
- Looks unprofessional. Large exporters hesitate to pay ₹19,777/month to a person, not a company.
- Cannot have a proper Terms of Service that holds up in court — because there is no registered legal entity.

**Verdict: Skip this completely.**

---

### OPTION B — OPC (One Person Company) ✅ RECOMMENDED FOR YOU

**What it is:** A real company — exactly like a Pvt Ltd — but owned by ONE person. Introduced in India by Companies Act 2013 specifically for solo founders like you.

**Legal status:** Separate legal entity. YOUR personal assets are protected.

**Cost to register:**
| Item | Cost |
|---|---|
| Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) | ₹2,000–3,000 |
| Government MCA filing fees | ₹3,000–5,000 |
| Professional (CA/CS) fees | ₹5,000–10,000 |
| **Total one-time** | **₹10,000–18,000** |

**Annual ongoing costs:**
| Item | Cost/year |
|---|---|
| Statutory audit (mandatory for OPC) | ₹8,000–15,000 |
| ROC annual filing (AOC-4 + MGT-7A) | ₹3,000–5,000 |
| Income tax return | ₹5,000–8,000 |
| GST filing (quarterly) | ₹8,000–15,000 |
| **Total annual** | **₹24,000–43,000 (~₹2,000–3,500/mo)** |

**What you get with OPC:**
- **Limited liability** — customer can sue the COMPANY, not you personally
- Separate bank account in company name (looks professional)
- Can sign contracts, Terms of Service is fully enforceable
- Certificate of Incorporation from MCA = your legal proof of business
- Company Identification Number (CIN) — put this on your Terms page
- PAN in company name → pay 22% flat tax instead of 30% personal slab
- Can convert to Pvt Ltd later if you want investors

**One restriction:** OPC annual turnover must stay under ₹2 crores and paid-up capital under ₹50 lakhs. If StoneVision grows beyond that — great problem to have — you simply convert to Pvt Ltd at that point.

---

### OPTION C — LLP (Limited Liability Partnership)

**What it is:** Needs MINIMUM 2 partners. Since you are solo right now, this requires adding someone (family member, friend) on paper.

**Cost:** ₹8,000–15,000 registration. Annual compliance: ₹10,000–20,000.

**Benefit over OPC:** Lower annual compliance cost. No mandatory audit below ₹40 lakh turnover.

**Problem for you:** You need a second partner. If you have a trusted family member (parent, sibling) who can be a designated partner on paper, LLP is cheaper to maintain. If not, stick with OPC.

## PART 2: HOW TO REGISTER OPC
### Step-by-step from your Mac M3 / Tab S9

---

**Step 1 — Get a Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)**
- You need a Class-3 DSC to digitally sign MCA documents
- Buy from: eMudhra (emudhra.com) or Sify (safescrypt.com)
- Cost: ₹2,000–3,000
- Takes: 1–2 days, video verification required
- Bring: Aadhaar, PAN, photo

**Step 2 — Apply for Director Identification Number (DIN)**
- This happens automatically when you file SPICe+ (Step 4)
- No separate application needed

**Step 3 — Reserve your company name**
- Go to: mca.gov.in → MCA Services → RUN (Reserve Unique Name)
- Name must be unique and end with "(OPC) Private Limited"
- Your name would be: **StoneVision AI (OPC) Private Limited**
- Check availability first: mca.gov.in → Master Data → Company Name Search
- Cost: ₹1,000 (government fee)
- Approval: 1–3 days

**Step 4 — File SPICe+ form (main incorporation form)**
- Go to: mca.gov.in → MCA Services → SPICe+
- Fill Part A (name reservation, already done) + Part B (full details)
- Attach:
  - MOA (Memorandum of Association) — what your company does
  - AOA (Articles of Association) — how your company is governed
  - Proof of registered office (your home address is fine — rent agreement + NOC)
  - Nominee's Aadhaar + PAN + consent (INC-3 form) — the nominee takes over if you die
  - Your PAN + Aadhaar
- Government filing fees: ₹0 for OPC with ₹1L authorized capital

**Step 5 — Receive Certificate of Incorporation**
- ROC (Registrar of Companies) reviews and approves: 3–7 days
- You receive: **Certificate of Incorporation** with your **CIN number**
- This is your most important document. Keep it forever.
- PAN and TAN are automatically generated and emailed

**Step 6 — Open a current account**
- Take your COI + PAN to any bank
- Open a current account in company name: "StoneVision AI (OPC) Private Limited"
- ICICI/HDFC/Kotak all have good digital current accounts

**Step 7 — GST Registration (if taking payments)**
- Go to: gst.gov.in → Registration → New Registration
- Required if annual turnover exceeds ₹20 lakhs (for services)
- You can register voluntarily even before that — looks professional
- Cost: Free
- GSTIN appears on all your invoices

**Step 8 — File INC-20A (Commencement of Business)**
- Must be done within 180 days of incorporation
- Proves you have deposited the authorized capital into the company bank account
- File on: mca.gov.in → Company Forms
- Government fee: ₹200

## PART 4: TRADEMARK REGISTRATION
### Protect the name "StoneVision AI"

This is SEPARATE from company registration. Company registration gives you a legal entity. **Trademark registration gives you exclusive rights to the brand name across India.**

Without trademark: Someone in Delhi can tomorrow open "StoneVision AI" and you have NO legal basis to stop them — even if you started first.

With trademark: You can send a legal notice and get a court injunction within days.

---

### What classes to file for StoneVision AI:

**Class 42** (most important) — IT services, SaaS, software, cloud services
- This protects "StoneVision AI" as a software service provider
- Cost: ₹4,500 (individual/startup rate, online filing)

**Class 35** — Business services, subscription-based commercial platforms
- This protects the subscription/marketplace aspect
- Cost: ₹4,500

**Total trademark cost: ₹9,000 in government fees + ₹3,000–5,000 CA fees = ₹12,000–14,000**

---

### How to file trademark (Step by step):

**Step 1 — Search first**
- Go to: ipindiaonline.gov.in/tmrpublicsearch/
- Search "StoneVision" in Class 42 and Class 35
- If nothing similar exists — proceed
- If something similar exists — consult a trademark attorney before filing

**Step 2 — Create account on IP India**
- Go to: ipindiaonline.gov.in
- Register as Individual (use your personal name — cheaper ₹4,500 rate vs ₹9,000 for companies)
- Important: File as individual BEFORE you incorporate the company — saves ₹4,500 in fees
- You can transfer/assign the trademark to the company later

**Step 3 — File Form TM-A**
- Fill applicant details, mark details ("StoneVision AI" word mark)
- Select Class 42 + Class 35
- Description: "Providing online SaaS platform for granite block measurement and cataloguing using AI vision technology"
- Pay ₹4,500 × 2 = ₹9,000
- Submit online

**Step 4 — You get an Application Number immediately**
- From this moment: Use ™ symbol next to your logo (e.g. StoneVision AI™)
- You have legal priority from the filing date
- Do NOT use ® until registration is granted (18–24 months later)

**Step 5 — Examination (3–6 months)**
- Trademark Registry examines your application
- May send examination report with objections — reply within 30 days
- Common objection: mark is descriptive. Response: it is a coined/invented term

**Step 6 — Publication in Trademark Journal (1–3 months after exam)**
- Anyone can oppose for 4 months
- If no opposition: Registration granted
- You receive Registration Certificate → now use ® legally

## PART 7: WHAT THIS ACTUALLY PROTECTS YOU FROM

### Scenario 1: Customer claims AI caused ₹5 lakh loss
**Without OPC:** They sue YOU personally. Your bank account, property at risk.
**With OPC:** They sue the COMPANY. Your personal assets are protected. Company's liability is limited to its assets.

### Scenario 2: Competitor opens "StoneVisionAI.in"
**Without trademark:** You can complain but have no legal basis to stop them.
**With trademark:** Send legal notice. Court injunction within days. Damages + criminal complaint possible (up to ₹5 lakh fine + 3 years imprisonment for infringer under Trade Marks Act).

### Scenario 3: Someone copies your code and resells it
**Your code is automatically protected by copyright** under Indian Copyright Act 1957 — no registration needed. Your Terms of Service (now backed by a registered company) explicitly prohibits this. You can send a cease-and-desist and approach the court.

### Scenario 4: Customer defaults on payment (doesn't pay ₹10,000/month)
**With OPC + GST + proper invoices:** You can file a case in commercial court. The paper trail (company invoice, GST record, bank statement) makes recovery straightforward.
